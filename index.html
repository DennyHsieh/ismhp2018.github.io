About ISMHP

 　　The purpose of 3rd International Symposium of Music and Health Promotion, ISMHP 2018, is to promote cross-disciplinary reasearch in music and medicine. Apart from conducting research on performance medicine, music psychology and related social issues, it will also discuss how music therapy can be applied to topics such as health promotion, music and health science and technology applications. The conference invites scholars and experts from domestic and oversea to publish papers on related topics such as “Music and Health Promotion”. Hope that through the combination of theory and practice. ISMHP 2018 will provide relevant people who focus on performing medicine, music therapy, music and health science and technology applications as an important opportunity in this academic exchange conference.

　　ISMHP 2018 inherits the experience of the previous two sessions and aims to promote cross-disciplinary research in music medicine. The conference will invite four international music medical scholars, including two performing medical research scholars and two music therapy research scholars:
　　Performing Psychology invited Prof. Kenny Dianna, Dept. of Psychology/Music, College of Arts and Social Sciences, University of Sydney. She has long focused on the “characteristics and treatment of anxiety in music performance” with methods in psychology, psychoanalysis and behavioral science research. Performing Physiology invited Prof. Bronwen Ackermann, Dept. of Biomedical Sciences, University of Sydney. She works as a physiotherapist and musician in anatomy of professional musicians, bones and muscles. Music Therapy invited Prof. Gottfried Schlaug, Dept. of Neurology, Harvard Medical School. He focuses on testing the long-term musical training (such as musical instrument practice) to promote brain plasticity to assist in the scientific research of rehabilitation of stroke patients.

　　第三屆音樂與健康促進國際研討會 (3rd International Symposium of Music and Health Promotion) 以「促進音樂與醫學的跨領域研究」為宗旨，除了進行演奏醫學、音樂生心理與相關社會議題之研討外，並將討論音樂療育如何應用於健康促進、音樂與健康科技應用等議題。本研討會徵求國內外學者專家，就「音樂與健康促進」等相關議題進行論文發表，期望透過理論與實務的結合，提供給關心演奏醫學、音樂療育、音樂與健康科技應用的相關人士，一個學術交流研討的重要機會。

 　　本次研討會承襲前兩屆舉辦經驗，以促進音樂醫學跨領域研究為目的，大會將邀請四位音樂醫學國際學者，包含兩位演奏醫學研究學者、兩位音樂治療研究學者：
 　　演奏心理學邀請雪梨大學藝術與社會科學學院心理學系/音樂系Kenny Dianna教授，她以心理學、心理分析和行為科學研究方法，長期專注於「音樂演奏焦慮的特性和治療」。演奏生理學邀請雪梨大學生物醫學科學Bronwen Ackermann副教授，目前從事職業音樂家、骨骼與肌肉解剖學的物理治療師及音樂家健康研究。音樂治療邀請哈佛大學醫學院神經內科Gottfried Schlaug副教授，主要研究集中於檢測長期音樂訓練(如樂器練習)促進大腦可塑性以協助腦中風病患復健之科學化研究。
